Shikha

This is Shikha’s creative corner—created by her, maintained by someone who is lucky enough to watch her bring these ideas to life. This gallery serves as a digital logbook of her artistic journey.

I’ve always admired the way Shikha looks at the world. She is as beautiful as she is passionate, and her art is a reflection of that gentle soul. She doesn’t draw to rush or to impress; she does it simply to pause, breathe, and enjoy the moment. I created this space to share the magic she creates . I hope you love her art as much as I do.
